## 1️⃣ THE PLATE PROPORTION SYSTEM (3×1 METHOD)

Every meal, divide your plate mentally into 4 equal parts:
- **3 parts (75%)** → **TYPE A foods** — burn fat, stabilize blood sugar
- **1 part (25%)** → **TYPE E foods** — raise glucose (not forbidden, just proportioned)

**TYPE A Foods:** All meats, fish, seafood, eggs, all cheeses, avocado, olive oil,
coconut oil, butter, nuts, and ALL vegetables that grow ABOVE ground.

**TYPE E Foods:** Bread, pasta, rice, tortillas, potato, sweet potato, yuca,
plantain, cereals, oatmeal, sugar, honey, sweets, chocolate, milk, fruit juices, sodas.

**Why it works:** Excess Type E → glucose spike → insulin flood → fat storage.
Limiting Type E to 25% → less insulin → body burns stored fat as fuel.

**Cultural examples:**

**Rule:** Never ask "can I eat this?" Ask "what proportion?"
