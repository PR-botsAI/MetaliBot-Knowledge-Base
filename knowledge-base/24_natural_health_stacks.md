# 🔗 Natural Health Stacks — Combining Everything for Maximum Results

> A "stack" is a combination of protocols, supplements, or interventions that work synergistically — the total effect is greater than the sum of parts. This guide shows how MetaliBot's complete knowledge base can be combined intelligently for specific goals.

---

## How to Think About Stacking

**The principle:** Each intervention works on a different mechanism. When multiple mechanisms are addressed simultaneously, results compound.

**The hierarchy:**
1. **Foundation first:** Fix the metabolic environment (diet, hydration, minerals)
2. **Remove blockers:** Candida, fatty liver, food aggressors, dehydration
3. **Amplify with protocols:** Intermittent fasting, morning shots, movement
4. **Accelerate with peptides:** Only effective when foundation is in place
5. **Maintain with lifestyle:** Sleep, stress, consistency

**Peptides work poorly when insulin is chronically high** — the receptor environment is compromised. Fix the diet first, then add peptides for acceleration.

## Stack 2: Diabetes Control Protocol

**Goal:** Reduce fasting glucose, lower A1c, reduce medication dependency (with physician)

**Foundation (non-negotiable):**
- 3×1 Diet strictly (Type S / Type F proportion)
- Remove personal food aggressors (use glucometer — 12-point rule)
- Adequate hydration (dehydration raises blood glucose)
- Candida cleanse (diabetics almost universally have Candida overgrowth)

**Daily protocol:**
```
MORNING (fasted):
- Morning shot: ACV (2 tbsp) + Lemon + Ceylon Cinnamon (½ tsp) + Ginger + Black Pepper
  → ACV + Cinnamon together = one of the most evidence-based glucose-lowering combinations
- Green juice: Nopal + cucumber + lemon (nopal specifically reduces post-meal glucose)

BEFORE MEALS:
- ACV shot (reduces postprandial glucose spike 19–34%)

WITH MEALS:
- Magnesium citrate
- Potassium

MOVEMENT:
- 20–30 min walk after largest meal (directly lowers post-meal glucose)

FASTING:
- Start with 12:12, progress to 16:8 as tolerated
- MONITOR GLUCOSE CAREFULLY — IF lowers glucose significantly
- Work with physician to adjust medications as glucose improves
```

**Monitoring:**
- Measure fasting glucose daily (before morning shot)
- Measure 1-hour post-meal glucose (identify food aggressors)
- A1c every 3 months to track progress

**Peptide addition (consult physician):**
- AOD-9604: Safe for diabetics (does NOT raise blood glucose unlike full GH)
- MOTS-c: Directly improves insulin sensitivity — highly relevant for diabetes
