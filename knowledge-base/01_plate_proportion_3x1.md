# 🍽️ The Plate Proportion System (3×1 Method)

## Core Principle

No calorie counting. No food elimination. No special products. Just proportions at every meal.

Divide your plate mentally into 4 equal parts:
- **3 parts (75%)** → Type A foods (Adelgazan — help burn fat)
- **1 part (25%)** → Type E foods (Engordan — raise glucose)

## Type A Foods — Fill 3/4 of Every Plate

**Proteins:**
- All meats: beef, pork, chicken, turkey, lamb
- All fish and seafood
- Eggs (all preparations)
- All cheeses

**Healthy fats:**
- Avocado
- Olive oil
- Coconut oil
- Butter
- Nuts: almonds, walnuts, macadamia, pecans

**Vegetables (above-ground only):**
Lettuce · Spinach · Broccoli · Cauliflower · Cucumber · Tomato · Onion · Peppers · Zucchini · Green beans · Celery · Cabbage · Kale · Chard · Asparagus · Mushrooms · Nopales

## Type E Foods — Limit to 1/4 of Every Plate

NOT forbidden — just proportioned.

Bread · Pasta · Rice · Tortillas (corn or flour) · Potato · Sweet potato · Yuca · Plantain · Name · Malanga · Cereals · Oatmeal · Granola · Sugar · Honey · Sweets · Chocolate · Cookies · Cakes · Milk · Fruit juices · Sodas · Beer · Sweet fruits in excess

## The Science

```
Excess Type E foods
  → Blood glucose spike
  → Pancreas releases insulin
  → Glucose + insulin converts to stored fat

Limiting Type E to 25%
  → Less glucose load
  → Less insulin
  → Less fat storage
  → Body burns stored fat as fuel
```

## Cultural Meal Examples

| Country | 3 Parts (Type A) | 1 Part (Type E) |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| 🇲🇽 Mexico | Carne asada + ensalada + nopales | 1 tortilla |
| 🇵🇷 Puerto Rico | Pollo + ensalada + habichuelas | 1 porción arroz |
| 🇨🇴 Colombia | Carne + ensalada + aguacate | 1 arepa |
| 🇺🇸 USA | Burger patty + cheese + salad | The bun only |

## The Golden Rule

> Never ask **"Can I eat this?"**
> Always ask **"What proportion?"**

## Why This Works Better Than Traditional Diets

- No forbidden foods → sustainable long-term
- Works with any cultural cuisine
- No measuring, no apps, no special products
- Addresses root cause (insulin/glucose) not symptoms
- Can be applied at restaurants, parties, family meals
