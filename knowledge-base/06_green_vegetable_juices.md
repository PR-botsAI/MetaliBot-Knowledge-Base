## Three Mechanisms That Make Vegetable Juice So Powerful

### 1. Bioavailable Micronutrients
Freshly extracted vegetable juice delivers vitamins and minerals in their **most absorbable form**. When you extract the juice and drink it immediately, the nutrients are fully bioavailable — no digestive degradation, no cooking loss.

A supplement capsule has to survive the digestive process first. Fresh juice bypasses all of that.

### 2. Natural Magnesium and Potassium via Chlorophyll
Vegetables are the most natural source of magnesium and potassium in their body-ready form. Chlorophyll — the molecule that makes plants green — contains magnesium at its center, just like hemoglobin contains iron at its center.

Drinking green juice = directly supplying magnesium and potassium to your cells in nature's original format.

### 3. Plant Exosome Communication (2016 Discovery)
**Dr. Mu et al. (2016)** discovered that plants communicate with intestinal cells through **exosomes** — tiny particles that carry genetic instructions.

These exosomes from plant cells tell your intestinal cells to:
- Repair damaged tissue
- Reduce inflammation
- Restore normal function

> *"Vegetable juice literally sends cellular repair signals to your body."*

This discovery explains why the clinical results from vegetable juices go far beyond simple nutrition.

Most commercial "healthy green juices" fail because they contain excess mango, pineapple, or banana. Read the label — if it has these in significant amounts, it's essentially a sugar drink.
