## THE PHILOSOPHY

This is not a diet. Not a detox. Not a supplement program. It is a **biological operating system** — a framework for running the human body at the level it was designed to perform.

**Three core principles:**
1. **Remove obstacles** — everything blocking optimal function (Candida, food aggressors, nutrient deficiencies, toxins, insulin resistance)
2. **Restore resources** — provide what the body needs to rebuild (minerals, hormones, cellular signals, sleep, movement)
3. **Amplify results** — use advanced tools (peptides, fasting, temperature therapy, light) to compound the gains

**The sequence matters:** Most people fail because they skip to step 3 without doing steps 1 and 2. Peptides don't work in a toxic, insulin-resistant, mineral-depleted body.

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Weeks 1–4)

*Remove the obstacles. Install the basics.*

### Daily Actions
```
UPON WAKING:
└── 16 oz filtered water immediately (rehydrates after overnight fast)
└── 10–30 min outdoor morning sunlight (circadian clock set; melatonin primed for tonight)

MORNING SHOT (before anything else):
└── 2 tbsp raw ACV + ½ lemon + ¼ tsp cayenne + pinch black pepper + ½ tsp Ceylon cinnamon + ginger
└── In 4 oz warm water; drink immediately
└── Wait 20 minutes before eating

MORNING WALK (fasted):
└── 30 minutes minimum
└── Capitalizes on overnight GH + morning cortisol + low insulin
└── Maximum fat burning window of the day

BREAKFAST (end of morning fast OR start of IF eating window):
└── 3×1 proportion ALWAYS: 3 parts Type A, 1 part Type E
└── Protein-forward: eggs, meat, cheese, avocado
└── Magnesium citrate (MagicMag®) with meal
└── Potassium capsules with meal

THROUGHOUT DAY:
└── Water: weight (lbs) ÷ 16 = glasses of water (minimum)
└── Urine target: pale yellow to clear

LUNCH:
└── 3×1 proportion
└── Magnesium + Potassium with meal

GREEN JUICE (afternoon or morning):
└── Spinach + cucumber + celery + lemon + ginger
└── Juicer/extractor only (not blender)

DINNER:
└── 3×1 proportion
└── Lighter on Type E foods (insulin sensitivity drops toward evening)
└── Stop eating 3 hours before bed (fasting window begins)

EVENING WIND-DOWN:
└── Dim lights after sunset (melatonin onset protection)
└── No screens after 9pm (or blue-light blocking glasses)

BEFORE SLEEP:
└── Magnesium citrate: 300–400 mg in warm water (2 hours before sleep)
└── Darkness: blackout curtains or sleep mask
└── Cool room: 65–68°F (18–20°C)
└── Target: 7–9 hours of sleep
```


## THE METABOLIC TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

*When results stall, work through this in order:*

### Problem: Weight Loss Stalled
```
1. Candida overgrowth? → Reassess symptoms list; if 7+, reinitiate cleanse
2. Food aggressors still in diet? → Re-test with glucometer; TAM elimination
3. Fatty liver? → Liver cleanse; reduce Type E foods further for 2 weeks
4. Hypothyroidism? → Morning temperature test 5 days; full thyroid panel
5. Sleep quality? → Track sleep; add sleep stack (Mg + tart cherry + DSIP)
6. Stress level? → Cortisol pattern test; add adaptogens
7. Dehydration? → Increase water to weight ÷ 12 (more than formula)
8. Insulin resistance deeper than thought? → Test fasting insulin; HOMA-IR
```

### Problem: Energy Low / Brain Fog
```
1. Candida toxins (acetaldehyde) — most common cause
   → Candida cleanse is step 1 before all other interventions

2. Sleep quantity/quality
   → Less than 7 hours = guaranteed brain fog regardless of anything else

3. Thyroid / T3 deficiency
   → Fatty liver blocks T4→T3 conversion
   → Liver cleanse → T3 restoration → energy returns

4. Magnesium deficiency
   → Cellular energy (ATP) requires magnesium
   → Increase MagicMag® dose; test RBC magnesium

5. Insulin resistance
   → Brain cells develop insulin resistance too (Type 3 diabetes)
   → Ketones bypass this; consider 16:8 IF or keto for 4 weeks

6. Vitamin D deficiency
   → Test 25-OH vitamin D; target 50–80 ng/mL
```

### Problem: Sleep Disrupted
```
1. Magnesium deficiency (2013 research: most insomnia = ATP crisis)
   → MagicMag® 400 mg 2 hours before bed

2. Cortisol too high at night
   → 4-point salivary cortisol test
   → Ashwagandha 600 mg evening
   → No intense exercise after 6pm
   → Blue light blocking glasses after sunset

3. Blood sugar crashing at night (waking at 3am)
   → Don't go to bed hungry
   → Small protein snack before bed (stabilizes glucose)
   → Check for hypoglycemia episodes

4. Candida toxins disrupting sleep architecture
   → Candida is active at night — toxin load disturbs sleep
   → Candida cleanse often dramatically improves sleep quality

5. For persistent insomnia despite all above:
   → DSIP peptide (sleep architecture restoration)
   → Tart cherry + L-Theanine + Glycine stack
```
